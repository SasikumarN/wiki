* How to choose one UML Software[fn:1]
UML不是画图，是创建一个模型。每家的产品都有各自的优势，所以要从使用的
角度选择。
+ Are you modeling or drawing? (Drawing - [[http://argouml.tigris.org/][ArgoUML]], free
  implementations, and [[http://office.microsoft.com/en-001/visio/][Visio]]
+ Do you want to formalize your modeling through profiles or
  meta-models? [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Object_Constraint_Language][OCL]]? ([[http://www.sparxsystems.com/][Sparx EA]], [[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IBM_Rational_Software_Modeler][RSM]], [[http://www.visual-paradigm.com/product/vpuml/][Visual Paradigm]])
+ Are you concerned about model portability, XMI support? ([[http://www.genmymodel.com/][GenMyModel]],
 [[http://www.sparxsystems.com/][Sparx EA]],  [[http://www.visual-paradigm.com/product/vpuml/][Visual Paradigm]], [[http://www.altova.com/umodel.html][Altova]])
+ Do you have an existing set of documents that you need to work with?
  (Depends on the documents)
+ Would you want to generate code stubs or full functioning code?  ([[http://www.genmymodel.com/][GenMyModel]],
 [[http://www.sparxsystems.com/][Sparx EA]],  [[http://www.visual-paradigm.com/product/vpuml/][Visual Paradigm]], [[http://www.altova.com/umodel.html][Altova]])
+ Do you need more mature processes such as use case management,
  pattern creation, asset creation, RUP integration, etc? (RSA/RSM/IBM
  Rational Products) 
+ Online ([[http://www.genmymodel.com/][GenMyModel]], [[http://yuml.me/][yUML]])
